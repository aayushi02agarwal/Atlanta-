Lifestyle

Atlanta is a plesant and vibrant city that offers expats a good quality of life and a relatively low cost of living. From suburbun family life in outer suburbs to the city chic lifestyle of Midtown, each neighbourhood of Atlanta has something unique to offer. It is also one of the greenest cities in the US with lush parks and even forests, which, along with a moderate climate, allow residents to enjoy an active outdoor lifestyle. There are also abundant shopping and nightlife opportunities throughout Atlanta.
