Festivals

Festivals in Atlanta range from high-energy dance parties and musical events to fine-art markets, family-friendly events, and cultural celebrations. Whether you’re interested in a low-key jazz performance on the lawn, a vibrant parade, or a weekend film festival, you’ll find it in Atlanta. Annual festivals can be found in Atlanta year-round, so there’s always something fun to do or see across the city–from springtime art and film festivals to summer music fests, Pride celebrations in autumn, and holiday parades in the winter.
